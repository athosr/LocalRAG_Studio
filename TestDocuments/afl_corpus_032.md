# ECN-2025-190 electrical acceptance (F-B12)

Before closing the F-B12 breaker after the Dec 2025 ice event, hold **Bus B** at or above **211 Vrms** for **10 minutes** continuously with no automatic tap changes.

If voltage sags below that threshold, abort and return to INC-2025-004 mitigation: stagger high Island-mode loads.
